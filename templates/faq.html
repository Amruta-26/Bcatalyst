FAQ

How do I check the current status of my order ?

Normally, it may take 2-3 working days for your order to get dispatched from our warehouse. As soon as the order gets dispatched from our warehouse, you will receive a confirmation message including the courier tracking information.

What is the estimated delivery time ?

After your order gets dispatched from our warehouse, it will take around 2-4 days to reach you. You can always keep a track of your order by the courier tracking details provided to you.

What are the payment methods accepted ?
